About Us

This website is dedicated to the glorious legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and the magnificent forts that played a vital role in building Swarajya.

Our aim is to provide historical information, images, and awareness about the forts built, captured, and protected by Shivaji Maharaj. These forts represent courage, strategy, and pride of Maharashtra.

This project is created by an shradhdaas an educational website to learn and practice HTML and CSS while preserving our history.
