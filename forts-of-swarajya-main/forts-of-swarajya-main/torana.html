-:TORANA FORT:-

Is one of the strongest fortresses on the Deccan Plateau and was historically referred to as Rairee or Rairy fort. Raigad Fort, a formidable hill fort in Maharashtra's Sahyadri mountains, served as the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who was crowned here in 1674. Known for its strategic importance, it features grand gateways like Maha Darwaja, royal palaces, a mint, water reservoirs, and the Jagdishwar Temple, accessible via about 1,700 steps or a ropeway. The fort showcases Maratha architectural brilliance with bastions, ramparts, and fortifications, offering panoramic views and symbolizing Maratha pride and resilience. Raigad Fort is famous as the historic capital of the Maratha Empire, established and fortified by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who was crowned king there in 1674, making it a symbol of Maratha pride, resilience, and the center of administration for his vast kingdom. Its strategic location, robust architecture (including the Maha Darwaja), and significant sites like the Raj Sadar, Queen's Palace, and Jagadishwar Temple, plus its panoramic views, add to its renown as the "Gibraltar of the East".
