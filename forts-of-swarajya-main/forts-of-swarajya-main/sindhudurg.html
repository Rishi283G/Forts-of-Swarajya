-:SINDHUDURG FORT:-

Sindhudurg Fort is a historic sea fort located off the coast of Malvan in Maharashtra. It was built in 1664 by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj to strengthen Maratha naval power. The fort is constructed on a rocky island in the Arabian Sea. It is known for its strong walls and massive stone architecture. Sindhudurg has hidden main entrances designed for defense. The fort covers an area of about 48 acres. Inside the fort, there are temples, water tanks, and ruins of houses. A temple dedicated to Shivaji Maharaj is present within the fort. The fort reflects brilliant military planning and engineering skills. Sindhudurg played an important role in protecting the Konkan coast. Today, it is a popular tourist and historical attraction. It stands as a symbol of Maratha bravery and maritime strength.
