-:RAJGAD FORT:-

Rajgad Fort is a historic hill fort located near Pune in Maharashtra. It served as the first capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort stands at an elevation of about 4,514 feet above sea level. Rajgad is known for its massive structure and strategic importance. The fort has three major machis: Padmavati, Suvela, and Sanjeevani. Balekilla is the highest point of the fort. Rajgad witnessed many important events in Maratha history. Strong fortifications, bastions, and water cisterns are present on the fort. The fort offers breathtaking views of the Sahyadri mountain range. Rajgad is a popular destination for trekkers and history lovers. It reflects the grandeur and planning of Maratha architecture. Rajgad Fort stands as a proud symbol of Maratha heritage.
