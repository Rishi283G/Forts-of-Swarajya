-:SHIVNERI FORT:-

Shivneri Fort is a historic hill fort located near Junnar in Pune district, Maharashtra. It is famously known as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1630. The fort stands at an elevation of about 3,800 feet above sea level. Shivneri has strong stone walls and several bastions for defense. The fort has a main gate called the “Maha Darwaja” and several other entrances. There are water tanks and temples inside the fort complex. The fort overlooks the surrounding valleys and trade routes, giving it strategic importance. It played a key role in Maratha military history. Shivneri Fort is a popular trekking destination today. The fort symbolizes the courage, vision, and legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It is well-preserved and maintained as a historical monument.
